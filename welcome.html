Getting Started

1

Open a New Tab

Click the "+" button or press Ctrl+T to open TabBoard as your new tab page.

2

Save Your Tabs

Use Ctrl+S to save all current tabs or click individual save buttons.

3

Use Command Bar

Press Ctrl+K to open the command bar for lightning-fast tab navigation.

4

Try AI Clustering

Let AI organize your tabs automatically by clicking the "AI Cluster" button.
